Business Plan


Starwood Trading LLC


1) Business line & what type of business ?


We are a trading company in UAE dealing primarily with African Teak for Indian market. We supply Plantation teak from Ghana to India. We supply teak to timber traders in Tuticorin, Mangalore, Coimbatore, Chennai , Mumbai , Gujarat etc.


2) How we operate?


When we take order from the timber traders in India and the payment terms will be Cash or transferable LC at Site.


1) They give advance 25% to start the process.


2) In the 2nd stage we send the container packing list to the customer to pay next 25% cash. So by this time we got the 50% payment, then we load the container to ship.


3)In the final stage we send copy of the shipping docs and receive the full amount. Then we send the original docs to our buyer.

If the payment is done in LC at site we receive payment from the buyers bank in each step. We take 10% to 25% advance in cash and balance make in transferable LC.


When we get money from our buyers we send money to our supplier co or our business associate in Ghana and he will export the material.


We have a reliable business associate or partner co in Ghana to serve our business deals.

5) Marketing: Scope, Profitability & Issues.


Teak always has great demand in India. Ghana teak has a competitive price than any country of origin teak.


a)Global, Domestic & Local market for the product.

In India normal grade teak has great demand. It is used for doors, furniture manufacturing & carpentry works. Globally Premium grade teak possess big demand. It is used in the furnishings of Luxury yatches, boats, Villas , Hotels etc.


b) Buyers & how would you approach them.

Direct customer meetings in there offices, digital app, Social media Promotions & other internet tools.


c) Is there is a gap in the market , you propose to fill ?

We can supply in the south Indian market. Where we have a great advantage than our competitors.


6) Risk Factors of the business.


Doing business without a reliable partner is the biggest risk factor from Africa. Shipment delay is another risk factor from African continent but Ghana till now do not possess that risk. Currency devaluation is another risk factor.


7) About the Operation or Associate Company activity from Africa.


We give advances to plantation companies and book our cargo. When we receive an order & advance from our buyers from India. We start the step by process to full fill the order as mentioned above.
